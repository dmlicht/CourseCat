What is Course Cat?

Course Cat is the answer to the question "Does anybody know a good online course or resource for learning [TOPIC]?" The app crowdsources good resources for self-directed learning and allows users to upvote and downvote the resources according to how effective they are in teaching various topics. Use, learn, vote, recommend your own courses, and enjoy!
